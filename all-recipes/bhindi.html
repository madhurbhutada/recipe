Bhindi Recipe

Description

Masala Bhindi, also known as spicy okra or ladyfinger, is a popular Indian Dish made with sliced okra cooked with a blend of spices. It is cooked until tender yet slightly crisp, making it a delicious side dish that pairs well with roti. Masala Bhindi offers a tasty way to enjoy okra with a hint of spice and aromatic flavours, making it a staple in Indian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Okra
  • Turmeric
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Oil
  • Coriander
  • Cumin
  • Roasted Peanut Powder
